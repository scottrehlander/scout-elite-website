Practice Planning lets you build a complete, timed practice session in minutes. The practice assistant drafts a plan for you — drawing on a drill library with rink diagrams and the takeaways from your recent game reports — and you fine-tune it from there. Print a clean sheet for the bench, run it live from your phone, or share it with your staff.

*Note: The free Starter plan includes 3 practice plans and 10 of your own custom drills, so you can run the full workflow. Solo and Pro unlock unlimited practice plans and drills. Sharing a plan with your team or staff is available on Pro.*

## Creating a New Practice Plan

1. Click **Plan a Practice** from your home screen, or open **Practice Plans** in the sidebar.
2. Click **+ New Plan**.
3. Set the basics — date, ice time available, number of skaters, and what you want the practice to accomplish.

You can save a group profile for a team, a player, or a skills cohort. Its defaults (typical duration, ice usage, age level) fill in automatically the next time you plan for that group.

## Drafting with the Practice Assistant

1. Open the **Practice Assistant** panel.
2. Describe the session in your own words — for example, *"60 minutes, full ice, 14 skaters, focus on breakouts."*
3. The assistant builds a complete, timed plan in a live preview, pulling from your drill library and the takeaways from your recent game reports.
4. Keep what fits and swap what doesn't. Ask for changes in plain English: *"add a small-area game at the end"* or *"shorten the warmup to 10 minutes."*

## Building the Plan by Hand

You can add and arrange items yourself at any time:

- **Drills** — pull from the drill library (see below).
- **Custom items** — anything that isn't a saved drill.
- **Notes** — reminders for yourself or the staff.
- **Sections** — group the practice into phases.
- **Stations** — group drills into a rotation, each with its own timing, for station-based ice.

Give every item a duration. The running total updates as you go, so your plan stays inside your ice time. Drag items to reorder them.

## The Drill Library

Open **Drills** in the sidebar to browse the library:

- **Community drills** come with rink diagrams, coaching points, and coach ratings. Give a thumbs-up to the ones worth running again.
- **Your own drills** — build a drill once and reuse it across every plan. Add a category, skills, age level, ice usage, equipment, coaching points, a rink diagram, and an optional video.

Add any drill to the current plan with one click.

## Diagrams

Each drill and plan item gets the same rink canvas as Playbooks — add players, pucks, lines, and arrows so the drill is easy to see, not just read.

## Print &amp; Run

- **Print** a clean, one-page sheet for the clipboard.
- **Run Practice** opens a live view on your phone so you can move through the plan block by block at the rink, timing and all.

## Sharing Practice Plans

*Note: Sharing requires a Pro tier subscription.*

Click **Share** on a plan to give access to individual coaches or your whole team. They see a clean, read-only view of the session, so the entire bench shows up knowing the flow.
