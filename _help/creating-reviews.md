Reviews are how you organize clips for structured team playback. Instead of scrolling through raw footage, you curate the most important moments into categories and present them in order.

> **This feature requires a Solo subscription.** [Learn about our plans](/help/subscription-plans/).

## Creating a Review

1. Navigate to **Reviews** in the sidebar
2. Click **Create Review**
3. Give your Review a name (e.g., "vs Eagles - Post-Game Review")
4. The Review wizard opens — follow the steps to add clips

## Adding Clips to a Review

During or after creation, you can add clips from any of your Clip Projects:

1. Browse your available clips
2. Select the clips you want to include
3. Assign each clip to a **category** — these are labels you define, like:
   - Offense
   - Defense
   - Special Teams
   - Individual Highlights
   - Areas for Improvement

## The Review Playback Experience

When you (or your team) open a Review, clips are organized by category:

- Click a category to see all clips in that group
- Clips auto-advance through the playlist so you can present without interruption
- Each clip plays back with its description and tags visible

This creates a professional, focused review experience — similar to what pro teams use, but simple enough for youth sports.

## Tips for Effective Reviews

- **Limit to 10-15 clips** — attention spans are short, especially with younger players. Focus on the most impactful moments.
- **Balance positive and corrective** — don't make reviews only about mistakes. Include great plays to reinforce good habits.
- **Use categories strategically** — start with team positives, then cover areas for improvement, and end on a high note.
- **Share in advance** — if your team has Pro tier, [share the Review](/help/sharing-content/) before the meeting so players can preview it.

## Sharing Reviews

On the Pro plan, you can share Reviews with individuals or your entire team. Shared Reviews are accessible to recipients in their own Reviews list. See [Sharing Videos, Clips & Reviews](/help/sharing-content/) for details.
