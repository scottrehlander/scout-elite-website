Welcome to Scout Elite — the sports video analysis platform built for coaches, parents, and players. This guide walks you through your first steps so you can start getting value from the platform right away.

## Creating Your Account

1. Visit [Scout Elite](https://xpress.scout-elite.com/) and click **Register**
2. Enter your email address and create a password
3. Check your inbox for a confirmation email and click the link to verify your account
4. Log in and you'll land on the **Dashboard**

> **Tip:** You can also use a magic link to log in — just enter your email on the login page and we'll send you a one-click sign-in link.

## Understanding the Dashboard

The Dashboard is your home base. When you first log in, you'll see quick-start cards that guide you through the main workflows:

- **Upload a video** — get your game footage into the platform
- **Create a Clip Project** — start building clips from your video
- **Create a Review** — organize clips for team review sessions

If you've been invited to any teams, pending invitations will also appear here so you can accept or decline them.

## Navigating the App

The sidebar on the left provides access to every area of Scout Elite:

- **Video Library** — upload, organize, and manage your video files
- **Clip Creator** — browse and manage your Clip Projects
- **Clip Search** — search across all your clips
- **Reviews** — create and manage team Reviews
- **Scout Elite Live** — view game reports from the mobile companion app
- **Analysis Hub** — visualize game event statistics
- **Account** — manage your profile, subscription, tag libraries, and teams

## Your Subscription

Scout Elite has three tiers:

| Plan | Price | What You Get |
|---|---|---|
| **Starter** (Free) | $0/mo | Browse the full app, view shared content |
| **Solo** | $4.99/mo | Upload videos, create clips & reviews, telestration, tags |
| **Pro** | $12.99/mo | Everything in Solo + sharing, teams, AI clip detection |

Both paid plans include a **14-day free trial** — no credit card required to explore the app, and you can start a trial when you're ready.

Features that require a paid plan show a small lock icon. Click it to learn more and start a trial.
