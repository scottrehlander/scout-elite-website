Your subscription controls which features you can access in Scout Elite. You can manage everything from your Account settings.

## Viewing Your Current Plan

1. Go to **Account** in the sidebar
2. Scroll to the **Subscription** section
3. You'll see your current plan, status, and billing period

## Upgrading Your Plan

To upgrade from Starter to Solo or Pro, or from Solo to Pro:

1. Go to **Account** → **Subscription**
2. Click **Upgrade**
3. Select your new plan
4. If you're eligible for a 14-day free trial, it will be offered automatically
5. Complete checkout via Stripe

Your new plan features become available immediately.

> **Tip:** You get a separate 14-day free trial for each tier you haven't tried before. If you've been on Solo and want to try Pro, you'll get a fresh Pro trial.

## Downgrading Your Plan

To downgrade from Pro to Solo:

1. Go to **Account** → **Subscription**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select the lower tier
4. Your downgrade takes effect at the end of the current billing period

## Cancelling Your Subscription

1. Go to **Account** → **Subscription**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Confirm the cancellation

Your access continues until the end of the current billing period. After that:

- You revert to the Starter (free) tier
- **Your data is preserved** — all videos, clips, reviews, and tags remain in your account
- You can re-subscribe at any time to regain access to paid features

## Promo Codes

If you have a promo code:

1. Go to **Account** → **Subscription**
2. Enter the promo code in the redemption field
3. Click **Redeem**

Valid promo codes grant immediate access to the corresponding tier without payment.

## Payment & Billing

Scout Elite uses **Stripe** for secure payment processing. We never see or store your full credit card number. Stripe handles all payment data securely.

- Billing is monthly
- You'll receive email receipts for each payment
- Failed payments will show a notification in the app
