Sharing is how you get your analysis in front of your team. You can share any content — videos, Clip Projects, or Reviews — with individual users or entire teams.

> **This feature requires a Pro subscription.** [Learn about our plans](/help/subscription-plans/).

## What You Can Share

| Content Type | What Recipients Can Do |
|---|---|
| **Videos** | Watch the video in their Video Library |
| **Clip Projects** | View the project and all its clips (read-only) |
| **Reviews** | Play through the organized review session |

All sharing is **read-only** — the original owner keeps full control. Shared content appears in the recipient's own navigation (their Video Library, Clip Creator, or Reviews list).

## Sharing with an Individual

1. Navigate to the item you want to share (video, Clip Project, or Review)
2. Click the **Share** button
3. Enter the recipient's email address
4. Click **Share**

The recipient will see the shared item in their app. They don't need a Pro subscription to view shared content — viewing is free.

## Sharing with a Team

If you've [created a team](/help/managing-teams/), you can share content with all team members at once:

1. Click the **Share** button on any item
2. Select the team from the dropdown
3. Click **Share**

Every current member of that team will see the shared item. If new members join the team later, they'll also get access.

## Who Can View Shared Content?

- **Individual shares** — only the person you shared with
- **Team shares** — all members of the selected team
- **Free users can view** — recipients don't need a paid plan to access shared content. This is great for parents and players who receive content from a coach with a Pro subscription.

> **Tip:** This is a powerful coaching pattern — one Pro user (the coach) can share content with an entire team of free users. Only the coach needs to pay.
