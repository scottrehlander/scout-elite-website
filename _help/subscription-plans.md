Scout Elite offers three subscription tiers designed to grow with your needs. Every user starts with full access to browse the app — paid features are unlocked as you upgrade.

## Free Trials

Both the Solo and Pro plans include a **14-day free trial**. During the trial you get full access to all features in that tier. If you don't cancel before the trial ends, your subscription will begin automatically.

> **Tip:** You can try Solo first, and later upgrade to Pro for a separate 14-day trial of the Pro features.

## How Feature Gating Works

Scout Elite doesn't hide pages behind a paywall. Free users can see the entire app and all navigation. When you encounter a feature that requires a paid plan, you'll see a small lock icon on the button. Clicking it opens an upgrade dialog where you can start a trial or subscribe.

This means you can explore everything before committing to a plan.

## How to Upgrade

1. Click any locked feature button, or go to **Account** → **Subscription**
2. Choose your plan (Solo or Pro)
3. If eligible for a free trial, you'll see the trial option
4. Complete checkout via Stripe (our secure payment processor)
5. Your subscription activates immediately

## Cancellation

You can cancel anytime from **Account** → **Subscription**. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period. After that, you'll revert to the Starter (free) tier but keep all your data.
