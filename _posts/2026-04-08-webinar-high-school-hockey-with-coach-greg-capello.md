## About Coach Greg Capello

Coach Greg (aka "Cap") brings a wealth of coaching experience at every level of high school hockey:

- **Current Varsity Head Coach** - Algonquin Regional High School (D1 MIAA)
- **Former Prep School Coach** - St. Mark's School and Worcester Academy
- **Owner of [X3 Hockey](https://www.x3hockey.com/){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"}** - providing skills sessions and coaching to youth hockey players

His unique perspective spanning prep school, public school, and youth development makes him the perfect person to break down what young players and their families need to know about high school hockey.

## What We'll Cover

This will be a candid conversation covering real topics that matter to players and parents:

- **What does it take to play high school hockey?** - Skills, commitment, and the intangibles coaches look for
- **Expectations at the high school level** - What changes from youth hockey and how to prepare
- **The difference between prep and public school hockey** - Insights from Coach Greg's experience at both levels
- **Player development** - What players should be working on now to be ready later
- **Coaching perspectives** - What high school coaches really want to see from incoming players
- **Tryout preparation** - How to show up ready and stand out for the right reasons
