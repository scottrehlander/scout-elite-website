How it Works

Scout Elite Live streamlines the entire video analysis process for coaches, athletes, and parents—making learning faster, more focused, and more impactful.

Live Game Event Clipping

Use the Scout Elite Live mobile app during games to instantly track goals, penalties, custom notes, and more. View game trends at a glance to direct your in-game strategy.

After the game, import these live-tagged events into your video sessions to automatically create clips, saving hours of painstaking manual review.

Targeted Review Sessions

Build focused review sessions for specific groups—like Defense or Goalies—so athletes only see what matters to them. This keeps sessions short, relevant, and engaging, maximizing learning and attention for every player.

Collaborate, Improve, Learn

Parents can easily share game videos with coaches for expert analysis. Once reviewed, results and feedback are shared back, giving families actionable insights to help their athletes grow and succeed.
